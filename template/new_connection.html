Steps for getting a New Service Connection
🏠 Getting a Domestic Connection
Thank you for choosing TNEB for your electricity services. Please carefully read the instructions before filling out the application form.
Click here for Online Registration
Step 1 : Documents Required ▶

(Mandatory for all applicants)

1. Proof of Identity (Any one):

Aadhaar Card / Voter ID / Passport / Driving Licence / PAN Card / Ration Card / Govt. Photo ID

2. Proof of Ownership / Occupancy (Any one):

Ownership certificate / Title deed copy / Land tax receipt (within 1 year) / Occupancy certificate

If tenant/occupier → NOC from owner + above document

3. Alternative Documents (Accepted for proof of ownership):

Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport / Ration Card / Residential Certificate from local body / Latest Water, Telephone, or Gas Bill

Step 2 : Charges Payable ▶

Application Fee: ₹50 (for LT connection)

Security Deposit + Service Connection Charges: As per TNEB estimate after inspection

If simple connection (≤20 kW, ≤35m, no poles needed) → package connection available, can pay full charges online

Step 3 : Inspection and Options ▶

Applicant can request a specific inspection date (extra fee may apply).

If applicant buys their own tested meter with certificate, monthly meter rent is not charged.

Step 4 : Final Submission ▶

Apply online → take printout of system-generated form → sign & attach photo.

Submit along with original documents at the time of inspection/service effecting.

Note: If false documents/consent used → TNEB can disconnect and forfeit all charges.

🏭 Getting a Non-Domestic Connection
Thank you for choosing TNEB. Please ensure you understand the steps before applying.
Click here for Online Registration
Step 1 : Documents Required ▶

1. Proof of Identity (Any one):

Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport / Driving Licence / PAN / Ration Card / Govt. Photo ID

2. Proof of Ownership / Occupancy (Any one):

Ownership / Possession certificate

Certified Title deed / Lease agreement

Property Tax or Land Tax receipt (within 1 year)

Occupancy certificate from local body

If Tenant → NOC from Owner + above document

3. Additional Requirements (Category-wise):

Agriculture: NOC/Letter from competent authority (Punja/Kole officer, Water dept if state-owned source)

Kiosks / Telecom Towers / Temporary structures: NOC from local body / land authority

Companies, Trusts, Institutions: Authorization/Resolution letter + ID proof

Industrial Estates/SEZ: Allotment letter (ownership proof not required)

Step 2 : Charges Payable ▶

Application Fee: ₹50 (for LT connection)

Security Deposit + Service Connection Cost: As per estimate after inspection

Package Connection Option (Quick service, ≤10kW load, ≤35m wiring, no poles):

Can pay full charges online (Application Fee + Security Deposit + Service Charges)

Connection may be given in 2 working days if no extra approval required

If poles/line extension needed → Charges decided after field inspection; applicant will get SMS/email and must pay before connection

Step 3 : Options for Applicants ▶

Can request a specific inspection date (extra fee applicable).

If applicant buys their own tested meter with certificate → No monthly meter rent.

Step 4 : Final Submissions ▶

Apply online → Take printout of application → Sign, affix photo, and submit with required documents at inspection/service time.

For CD-based billing (higher loads) → Service Connection Agreement on non-judicial stamp paper required.

Note:False/forged documents → Immediate disconnection + forfeiture of amount paid + liability for losses.

User Guide - How to Use TNEB Web Portal

Welcome to the TNEB Web Portal, designed to provide convenient, secure, and quick access to your electricity services. Explore the step-by-step instructions below to make the most of the portal:

1. Registering a Complaint or Service Request

  • Click on "Register Complaint/Service" on the homepage or menu.
  • Select the appropriate Complaint/Service Type (e.g., Line Related, Billing Issue).
  • Fill in details including Category, Contact Person name, Mobile Number, Landmark, and precise Problem Description.
  • Enter your Consumer Number (found on your electricity bill).
  • Select your District and Section for location-based routing.
  • Complete the CAPTCHA verification.
  • Submit the complaint. You will receive a reference number to track the issue status.

2. Quick Pay - Online Bill Payment

  • Use the "Quick Pay" option for instant bill payment without logging in.
  • Enter your Consumer Number and verify your bill details.
  • Choose payment method: UPI, Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, or popular wallets (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm).
  • Complete your payment and save the digital receipt for your records.

3. Online Services

  • Access service requests such as Load Change, New Connection, Ownership Transfer.
  • Follow application prompts, upload required documents, and submit with applicable fees.
  • Track application status in real-time directly within the portal.

4. Register Mobile/Email

  • Link your mobile number and email ID to receive timely bill alerts, outage notifications, and updates.
  • This helps the TNEB communicate efficiently and minimize interruptions.

5. Track Complaint Status

  • Go to "Complaint Status" from the site menu.
  • Enter your complaint reference number provided during registration.
  • View updates on complaint resolution progress.

6. How to Register and Login

  • Use the Sign Up option to create a new user account.
  • Enter your Consumer Number, mobile number, and email ID as requested.
  • Set a secure password and verify your identity via OTP received by SMS.
  • Login using your User ID and password to access personalized services.

7. Additional Tips & Customer Support

  • Keep your Consumer Number handy for quick reference during payments and complaints.
  • Never share your passwords or OTPs to protect your account security.
  • For assistance, call the TNEB 24x7 Helpline: 1912 or 0471-2555544.
  • Download the official TNEB Mobile App for convenient access and alerts on the go.

8. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Can I pay my bill without logging in? Yes, use the “Quick Pay” option by entering your Consumer Number.
  • Where do I find my Consumer Number? On your electricity bill, listed under consumer details.
  • How long does complaint resolution take? Resolution time varies; typically issues are addressed within 24-48 hours depending on severity.
  • Can I use e-wallets for payment? Yes, major UPI and wallet payments like Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm are accepted.
  • Is there a mobile app available? Yes, available for Android and iOS via official app stores.
